## About Oxford Advanced Learning

Oxford Advanced Learning (OAL) is a company initiative dedicated to providing premium services for the educational sector. These services include consultancy for university applications, private tutoring with a team of subject specialists, and software products that help teachers and schools in the recruitment process (TeacherSearch) and in advanced digital learning (IRMaths). OAL's software products are at the frontier of modern pedagogy, incorporating advanced AI capabilities.

Oxford Advanced Learning works in close partnership with IREducation, an innovative education company based in Hong Kong, operating in China and the UK. Together, we are building a complete ecosystem that redefines the way students, teachers, and institutions engage with education.

With a strong focus on Mathematics, Sciences, and university preparation, our long-term vision is to become the leading educational corporation in the UK — an all-encompassing "educational emporium" offering tools for students, teachers, and institutions.
