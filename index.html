About Us

Robert Hainitz

Based in Oxford, UK | Founder, Oxford Advanced Learning

For the second part of my professional career, I have been an accomplished physics and mathematics teacher and lecturer with a proven track record. Before this, I was a highly successful executive in the ICT technical support business, managing organisations of over 300 people. I combine commercial know-how with excellent operational and people management skills, and I am a conceptual, strategic thinker with advanced analytical capabilities who employs both commercial and scientific methodologies to achieve operational goals and business targets.


I am the founder of Oxford Advanced Learning (OAL), a company initiative dedicated to providing premium services for the educational sector. These services include consultancy for university applications, private tutoring with a team of subject specialists, and software products that help teachers and schools in the recruitment process (TeacherSearch) and in advanced digital learning (IRMaths). OAL’s software products are at the frontier of modern pedagogy, incorporating advanced AI capabilities based on Google’s Gemini platform.


Oxford Advanced Learning works in close partnership with IREducation, a dynamic company founded by one of my former prodigy students.

Partnership with IREducation


IREducation (InteRactive Education) is an innovative education company based in Hong Kong, operating in China and the UK, where the latter is run under Oxford Advanced Learning. We are building a complete ecosystem that redefines the way students, teachers, and institutions engage with education.

With a strong focus on Mathematics, Sciences, and university preparation, our long-term vision is to become the leading educational corporation in the UK — an all-encompassing “educational emporium” offering tools for students, teachers, and institutions.

We aim to develop and deploy a suite of AI-driven tools, state-of-the-art digital platforms, and high-quality curricular content aligned with national standards. This initiative is designed not only to fill current educational gaps but to anticipate and shape the future of learning through innovation, accessibility, and academic excellence.
